4. **Next Steps**: Once selected, we'll customize with your business details

## 🎯 Next Steps After Selecting Design

Once you choose your favorite design, we will:

1. **Customize Content**
   - Replace sample text with your business information
   - Add your actual services and menu items
   - Update contact details and contact information

2. **Setup Google Sheets Integration**
   - Create a Google Sheet for menu management
   - Olav can add/update menus without touching code
   - Website automatically displays the latest menus

3. **Domain & Hosting**
   - Register your chosen domain (olavlagos.com, olav.food, etc.)
   - Deploy to Netlify (free hosting)
   - Connect custom domain

4. **Email Setup**
   - Configure Zoho Mail for professional email (@yourdomain.com)
   - Enable Olav to send professional emails

5. **Go Live**
   - Website goes live on your custom domain
   - Olav has full control to manage menus and content
